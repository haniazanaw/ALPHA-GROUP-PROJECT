🌿 Habitat Hoppers: Game Rules

Learn how to play and win!

🎯 Objective

Be the first player to reach the FINISH tile by moving across the game board, overcoming challenges, and using your animal’s special powers wisely!

🎲 Game Components

  • Deck of Cards: Animal, Red (danger), and Green (positive action)
  • Dice: Roll to determine steps
  • Tokens: Player pieces in different colors
  • Game Board: 32 tiles of various types

🃏 Deck of Cards

Animal Cards (16)

4 animals per habitat: Ocean, Desert, Savannah, Forest

Red Cards (6)

Danger cards! Pick one when you land on a red tile and follow the action.

Green Cards (16)

Positive actions! Example: “Move three tiles ahead”.

🎮 How to Play

  1. Each player chooses a token and places it on the START tile.
  2. Players take turns rolling the dice and move forward accordingly.
  3. Follow the tile instructions (pick cards if applicable).
  4. Use your animal’s power once during the game strategically.
  5. The first player to reach the FINISH tile wins!

📏 Game Rules

  • Players: 2–6
  • Each animal has a unique power used only once per game.
  • If you land on an animal tile, swap your animal with a new one.
  • If a player blocks a tile, others behind must skip their turn.
  • Green Tile: Pick a green card.
  • Red Tile: Pick a red card.
