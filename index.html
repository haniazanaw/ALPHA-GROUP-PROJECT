HABITAT HOPPERS

Welcome to the most magical place on earth!

Hoppers is an ecology-themed game. It is helpful to understand the different animals and their habitats,
as well as some of the unique characteristics they
possess. Players must move their animals from
the starting point to the finish line. A player who
reaches the finish line first becomes the winner.
